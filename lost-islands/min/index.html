The Lost Islands

v1.0 By Luke @deathraygames for js13k 2017

A cataclysm has shattered the known world and left islands adrift in the cosmos. Each island has only a handful of inhabitants, struggling to survive. All signs of civilization are gone, except for a few ancient shrines and stone gateways, and only the mystics seem to understand their signifigance.

You wake up on one of these islands as a Stranger, not remembering your past. Perhaps with some exploration and meditation you will be able to uncover a way home...

You enter the red moon gate and teleport home.

Tips

  • Move with arrow keys or WASD
  • [I] or [Tab] to toggle this inventory
  • [E] or [Space] will do actions when near people or objects
  • [M] to meditate
  • [+] & [-] to zoom
  • Boats, coats, and water will increase your speed on the seas, mountains, and deserts
  • Tents will speed up time [E] when not near anything
  • Your goal is to explore: find all shrines, a mystic, and a way to leave

FAQ

How to play:

Select one of the tabs above to determine your space empire's current "X" focus (as in "4X"). Yes, that's all you can do. You'll need to change focus many times to properly expand to a thousand suns.

Why is this game so simple?

In general incremental games are not particularly complex to play, plus this game is made to be especially simple for the Simple Jam.

Why isn't there a save or [my favorite feature]? Why do things not work right?

This is just an early alpha.

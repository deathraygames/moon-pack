StarDust.js
A Universe in 13kb
"Every atom in your body came from a star that exploded... You are all stardust. ... The stars died so that you could be here today."
- Lawrence Maxwell Krauss

Story: You play a small, dense planetoid, recently born from a dying star. Remarkably, you have the abilities to propel yourself through space and create more stardust at will.

Primary Goal: Enjoy the universe. Have fun doing whatever you want in the galactic sandbox. Secondary Goal: Develop life using the basic elements of this universe: earth, water, air, and fire (heat).

How to Play: Use w/a/s/d or arrow keys to move your planetoid through space. Impacting other objects will transfer earth/water/air to whichever object is more massive. Try to only hit smaller objects, and avoid contacting massive objects and suns.
Press [Space] to generate stardust around you. Press [e] and [t] to toggle element and temperature sensor readings, and [o] to toggle object information. Press [h] to return to this help screen and [p] to pause. Press [F11] to go fullscreen.

Hit [Enter] to Begin
